Getting to
Montauk

{% include svg/car.svg %}
Car
Montauk is approximately a 2.5 hour drive from New York City.
{% include svg/bus.svg %}
Bus

Take the Hampton Jitney Bus from New York City, JFK, LaGuardia or MacArthur Airport.

The Jitney costs roughly $30 and takes around 4 hours.

More Info

{% include svg/train.svg %}
Train

Take the Long Island Rail Road (LIRR) from either Penn Station or Atlantic Terminal.

Cost is roughly $25 each way and takes about 2 hours.
